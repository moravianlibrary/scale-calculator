No scale on the antique map:

  1. In the first field select the system used by the author of the antique map,
  2. Measure the distance between two selected points on the antique map (in milimeters) and enter it to the second field,
  3. Mark out the same points on the map below,
  4. The number will be taken automatically and will appear in the third field (in kilometers)
  5. The scale in the 1: … format appears below.

Graphic scale (a line segment) on the antique map:

  1. In the first field select the system used by the author of the antique map,
  2. Measure the distance between two selected points on the antique map (in milimeters) and enter it to the second field,
  3. Enter the number of units on the graphic scale to the third field,
  4. Select units used by the author of the antique map in the fourth field,
  5. The scale in the 1: … format appears below.
